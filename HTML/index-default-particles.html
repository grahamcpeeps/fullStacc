Pricing

At fullStacc, our services are split into three levels of complexity. Each plan starts at a base price, but can vary slightly depending on the amount of hours required to complete the project as well as the amount of detail requested by the client.

  • Basic
  • Starting at $200
  • The basic plan is meant for those who want a simple website without excessive visual features/graphics. This option is best for those looking to create a website which is more focused on the information on the page, such as contact information.
  • Standard
  • Starting at $500
  • The standard plan is our most frequently desired plan for those who want a more visually appealing website that elegantly displays your content while providing more functionality than a basic website. While most of these types of sites are still one page, this option is best for those who want an eye-catching webpage that can draw in more potential clients/users with just a quick glance.
  • Advanced
  • Starting at $1200
  • The advanced plan is aimed towards those with more complex requests for their site. This can include dynamic backgrounds, interactive content, and more complex styling techniques such as parallax scrolling.
  • Logo Design
  • $75
  • In addition to designing a website for you, our team can design your company or business a personalized logo to represent your organization and complement the design of the site.
